Dirt Cake

Description

This deliciousc cake looks like like dirt and taste like heaven! This cake utilizes gummy worms to imitate real dirt characteristics!

Ingredients

cake

  • 1 box of chocolate cake mix
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 and 1/3 cups of vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs

dirt topping

  • 4 oz pudding mix
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups whipped cream
  • 2 cups crushed cookie
  • 2 cups hummy worm
  • 1/4 cip chocolate fudge sauce

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients into a large bowl
  2. Pour 5 cups of water into the trash can
  3. Let the trash can sit for 2 days
  4. Collect some sticks from outside and place them into the trash can until it is full
  5. Try to remove trash bag from trash can. If its too easy, add 5 more cups of water
  6. Drop the bag and use whatever space is left in the bowl to try and collect the trash
  7. Eat the trash
